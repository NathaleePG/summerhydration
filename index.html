Summer Hydration

May 25th, 2018

Hey guys! Today, I’m going to talk to you about my current favorite obsession this summer and as you can already quite see, it’s naturally flavored water. But before I go on about this, I first want to talk to you guys about water and why it’s so important to drink those 8 glasses every single day.

Why it’s SO important:

About 60% of our body is composed of water which is why it’s so important to keep ourselves hydrated or else various bodily functions become inefficient or in worse cases, fail to generate. Water regulates our body temperature and body pH, helps metabolize various bodily hormones and important nutrients/minerals, keeps our skin, hair and nails hydrated and keeps the blood running through our veins. Water is literally LIFE, so let’s make it a habit to drink those 8 glasses each day!

I know, I know, it’s easier said than done. The thought of it is pretty simple but because of our busy schedules or perhaps our habits that refrain us from grabbing a glass or two of water as we head towards a soft drink or even nothing (haha! Trust me, I’ve been there), it’s hard to stay on top with our water count! Girl! Boy! I know! But believe me, if you start taking the baby steps now, you’ll eventually get there and your life will change…FOREVER! You’ll feel less tired, more energized, your skin will clear and your mind will brighten up with happier and healthier thoughts. Have I convinced you yet? If I have then let’s get to it!

The Ritual:

Water after a run is delicious and refreshing just as it is. But adding a bit of natural flavor to it can really make that sip go from mmm to DANG! All I add to my water are a few slices of my favorite fresh and citrus fruits such as lime, oranges and cucumbers! Cut up a few slices, add it into your glass with a few ice cubes and BAM, you got yourself a classy glass of H2O!

You can add any fruit you like to your drink! Sometimes I like adding strawberries or lemon in there too but anything you got in your fridge will always work! Hydrate your body with those sexy electrolytes! ;) Yasss!!

Anyways, I hope you guys enjoyed this little blog post! It was so much fun! Stay tuned for more blog posts coming out soon and as always, cheers to health and clear skin!
